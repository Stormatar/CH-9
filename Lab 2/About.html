About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered non profit wildlife rescue rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitator Association

Watch the video below to meet our new, rescued friends.

We help many animals, such as

  • Racoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
